# Strengths Reflection

A private, evidence-backed strengths reflection for designers, built for review-cycle prep. Ten questions, plus whatever review material you have access to, and you get a strengths snapshot that only you see and only you keep.

It grew out of the studio's career research: growth is hard to see, and how well it goes depends too much on which manager and projects you land. This tool puts the reflecting half of that loop in your own hands.

## What you get

A markdown snapshot, built from your answers and your record, covering:

- The work that energizes you and the work that drains you, stated plainly.
- How you do your best work with others, and what teammates seek you out for.
- Three to five strengths, each tied to specific evidence, organized with the CliftonStrengths four-domain lens (Executing, Influencing, Relationship Building, Strategic Thinking) in plain language.
- Where your record sees more in you than you claim, when you share review material. In practice, that gap almost always runs in your favor.
- What you want to grow next, in your own framing, never someone else's.
- Three talking points you could say out loud in your next review conversation.

## Setup

Pick whichever path matches how you use Claude. All three behave the same.

**Claude project (works on any plan)**
1. In Claude (claude.ai or the desktop app), create a new Project. Call it "Strengths Reflection."
2. Open the project's custom instructions and paste in the full contents of [`strengths-reflection/SKILL.md`](strengths-reflection/SKILL.md).
3. Open the project and say hi.

**Skills (if your plan shows Skills under Settings, then Capabilities)**
1. Add a new skill using the `strengths-reflection` folder from this repo.
2. Start a chat and ask for a strengths reflection.

**Claude Code**
1. Copy the `strengths-reflection` folder into `~/.claude/skills/`.
2. Run `/strengths-reflection` in any session.

## Before you start

Have nearby whatever you're comfortable sharing: past self reviews, review packets, peer feedback, kudos messages. All optional; the reflection works without them, but a second source makes the read noticeably stronger. Don't paste client-confidential material or other people's reviews.

Takes about 25 minutes. Best done a week before your review conversation, not the night before.

## Privacy

Nothing you write is stored anywhere or sent to anyone. The conversation happens in your own Claude account, the snapshot is a markdown file you save yourself, and sharing any of it (with your manager, in your review, with no one) is entirely your call.

## What this is not

It uses the CliftonStrengths domains as an organizing lens, in plain language. It is not the official CliftonStrengths assessment, it never assigns Gallup theme names, and it is not a performance evaluation or a psychological assessment of any kind. It's a structured way to see your own evidence clearly.
