Strengths
Reflection

A private, evidence-backed read of your strengths, built for review-cycle prep. Ten questions, your own record, one snapshot only you see.

Get the skill on GitHub Setup takes 5 minutes. The reflection takes about 25.
How it works
01

Set it up in Claude

One file, three ways in: paste it into a Claude project's instructions, install it as a skill, or drop it into Claude Code. The repo README walks you through whichever path matches how you already use Claude.

02

Answer ten questions, bring your record

Questions about what energizes you, how you work with others, and what you want to grow next, one at a time, at your pace. Share whatever you have access to: self reviews, review packets, peer feedback, kudos threads. All optional, all private.

03

Keep the snapshot

You get a markdown document that's yours to save, revise, and reuse: your strengths with the evidence behind them, and three talking points you could say out loud in your next review conversation.

What it surfaces
  • The work that energizes you, and the work that drains you.Stated plainly, so it can guide which projects you raise your hand for.
  • How you do your best work with others.Your collaboration shape, and what teammates actually seek you out for.
  • Where your record sees more in you than you claim.When you share review material, the gap almost always runs in your favor. Most of us undersell ourselves; the evidence usually disagrees.
  • What you want to grow next, in your own words.The tool never introduces a weakness you didn't name yourself.
Private by design

Nothing you write is stored anywhere or sent to anyone. The conversation happens in your own Claude account, the snapshot is a file you save yourself, and sharing any of it is entirely your call. It's not a performance evaluation, and it isn't visible to managers, ever.

A resource from the design studio, built on our career research about making growth visible. It organizes evidence with the CliftonStrengths four-domain lens in plain language; it is not the official CliftonStrengths assessment.
